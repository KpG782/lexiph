## Tips for Creating Reports

1. **Start with the template** - Copy the complete template above
2. **Use consistent emojis** - Stick to âœ…, âš ï¸, ğŸš« for sections
3. **Group by severity** - Put all compliant items together, then warnings, then critical
4. **Be specific** - Include section numbers, legal references, and action items
5. **Use bold for labels** - `**Status:**`, `**Finding:**`, `**Reference:**`
6. **Add horizontal rules** - Use `---` to separate major sections
7. **Include metadata** - Document name, date, query at the top
8. **End with disclaimer** - Always include legal disclaimer at bottom
